Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Foundation.

How can I donate instruments?

We accept guitars, drums, keyboards, and wind instruments in working condition. You can drop them off at our headquarters in Bogota or contact us to schedule a pickup.

Where do the donations go?

100% of the donations go directly to supporting music education for underprivileged children in the local community. We are fully transparent with our finances.

Can I volunteer as a teacher?

Yes! We are always looking for passionate musicians to join our teaching staff. Please send your CV and portfolio to volunteer@foundation.org.

Is the foundation tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a registered non-profit organization. We provide official certificates for all monetary and equipment donations for tax purposes.
